Suriya Gunasekar

Research Assistant Professor
Toyota Technological Institute at Chicago
 

I am a research assistant professor at TTI Chicago. I received my PhD in 2016 from The University of Texas at Austin, where I was advised by Prof. Joydeep Ghosh. Past affiliations: WNCG and IDEA Lab @ UT Austin.

Research areas: machine learning and optimization. Below are some links for more overview on my research (updated Jan 2019).
